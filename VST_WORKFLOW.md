This guide explains the **uniform workflow philosophy** of SoundFirst PRO for supported VSTs.  
Instead of memorizing countless plugin layouts, knobs are standardized so you can mix by **muscle memory**.

> **Golden Rule:**  
> * **Knob 1 (K1)** is always the "Driver" (Threshold, Input, or Gain).  
> * **Knob 2 (K2)** is always the "Level" (Makeup, Output, or Volume).  
> * **Shift + Knob** accesses secondary parameters.  
> * **Touch Knob üñêÔ∏è** solos the parameter or band if supported.
